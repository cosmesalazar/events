The Main Event

The Largest Public
Via Ferrata in the US.

Ashley Gorge Via Ferrata isn't a walk in the park. It's a full-body climb through one of Utah's most dramatic canyon systems — iron rungs, cables, and ledges carved into sheer rock face, hundreds of feet above the canyon floor.

Via Ferrata (Italian for "iron road") combines the thrill of technical climbing with accessibility — no ropes to rig, no anchor-setting skills required. Just your harness, your nerve, and the view.

Guided Via Ferrata experiences often run $250+ per person. We've locked in gear rental at $45/person — less if you bring your own.
